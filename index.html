WHAT IS SRI LANKA KNOWN FOR?

Sri Lanka is known for its unique biodiversity, huge cinnamon and tea exports,
and breathtaking natural beauties! It's also known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean,
and the country is rich in cultural and religious diversity.
