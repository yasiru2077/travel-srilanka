Childrens wellbeing

We support children primarily through child sponsorship. By 2022, our efforts will have helped 6,400 sponsored children and 4,000 supported children gain access to education, establish their lives, and find work. Our nonprofit operates 8 centers and 80 boarding houses and supports roughly 96 development initiatives (such as schools or student housing) each year. One of our basic goals is to keep administrative expenditures to a minimum so that your gifts have the greatest possible impact. This is demonstrated by our excellent philanthropic expenditure rate of 82%. When you sponsor a child in srilanka, you and your sponsored child form a bond through the exchange of letters.You're simply giving someone in Sri lanka a life-changing gift.
