Rumassala

Ravana It was necessary to bring herbals from the Himalayas when Rama's brother Lakshman was nearly killed in battle. Hanuman, the monkey god, has taken on the mission, but he has forgotten the herbs' names. Hanuman shattered a section of one mountain (Holy Sanjeevi) where herbs were found, then threw the rest of the mountain away after obtaining the herbs. Rumassala arose from this shattered portion. Rumassala's colonial name, Buno Vista, means "good view."This tranquil location is a must-see on your stay to Galle! The greatest time to visit is in the evening, when the sun has set and the floor has become colder.
